Target Audience

The target audience of the DarkSkyWeather.com web site is described below:

  • Who: It can be used by anyone who is looking to find out about the weather. The people who mainly use this service are people who are booking holidays to check the weather in a different country. It is also used by anyone who wants to go on a fun day out.
  • Age: 10 to 100
