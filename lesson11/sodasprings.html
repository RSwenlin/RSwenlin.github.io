The Caribou County Fair

Come and See 2021! That is the theme of the Caribou County Fair. All residents of Caribou County are invited-bring your friends too. The night starts out on Monday with Team Roping in the arena. The Round Up will be Tuesday night @7pm. We can't forget the kids! Wednesday is kids day. 9am-7pm in the Rodeo Arena. The childrens parade starts @4:30pm. Thursday and Friday is all about the Rodeo, come check it out. We saved the best for Saturday! The 5K starts bright and early @7:30A.M. The Big Parade is @5:30 on Saturday. Free candy throwing for the kids. We have another special treat a Hypnotist! You can get in on that action Wednesday through Saturday. It is a guarantee fun time for all. Bring a picnic, or purchase food from our vendors. Camp trailers and campers needing to be parked by necessity on the fairgrounds during fair week will be required to have a parking pass and will be given an assigned space.
